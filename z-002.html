Z-002
Class: Paperweight ☐

Containment: Standard shelf. Recording device recommended. Limit exposure >5 min. Level 2 personnel only.

Description: Broken mantel clock. Glass cracked. Brass tarnished.
Emits whispers within ~1 m. Phrases match previous operatives — some ██████. Repetition observed.

Behavior:
Whispers at irregular intervals. Sometimes stops. Then starts again.
Repeats numeric sequences hourly. Non-temporal.
Patterns may link to other Nulls.
Notes:

“I hear words I never said. Z-002 remembers more than memory allows.” — Dr. M. Harel

Incident Log (Excerpt):
Operative ███ reported dizziness after 10 min. Whispers included directives from ██████.
Sequence repeated at 02:00. No further anomalies recorded.
  
Logs:

Log 1
Glass cracked. Whispering begins once the room is silent. Words familiar, but from ██████. Operative ███ noted faint disorientation.

Note: “Sounds like a voice I know. Can’t remember who.” — Harel


Log 2
Brass tarnished. Clock hands frozen. Whispering louder when nobody is near. Abrupt silence when approached.

Note: “It’s louder when you’re not listening. How does that make sense?”


Log 3
Noise reported at 02:17. Overlapping phrases. Operative ███ covered ears; symptoms: mild nausea. ██████ repeated three times.

Note: “Recording shows nothing. Room mic flatlined.”


Log 4
Contained in soundproof box. Whispers persist. No vibrations detected. Operative ███ suggested disposal. Request denied.

Note: “If it talks through silence, how do we stop it?”


Log 5
Exposure: 7 minutes. Whispering unintelligible, fragmented. Occasional word matches older reports.

Note: “I heard my name this time.”


Log 6
Unattended for 3 hours. Whispering captured faintly at 03:44. Recording cuts mid-phrase.

Note: “Playback corrupted. It’s always that word that breaks it.”


Log 7
During maintenance, whispering spiked. Operative ███ described presence behind them. No one nearby.

Note: “He swore someone exhaled on his neck. Cameras show static.”


Log 8
Transported to secondary storage. Whispering minimal. Operative ███ experienced dizziness mid-route. Object silent since arrival.

Note: “I think it’s listening to where it is.”
