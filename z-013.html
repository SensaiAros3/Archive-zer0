Z-013

Object Class: Eclipse ◎

Containment Status: Restricted Auditory Observation (Soundproof Chamber)

Containment Procedures:
Z-013 is contained in a soundproofed chamber with vibration dampeners.
Observers must wear auditory isolation equipment.
Unauthorized vocalization near the chamber is prohibited.

Description:
The Silent Choir emits inaudible harmonic vibrations that influence
emotions and thought patterns. Exposure can cause uncontrollable
emotional responses and altered decision-making.

Backstory:
Discovered in 2021 in an abandoned cathedral in Prague, the Choir’s effects
were noted when visitors felt inexplicable sadness or euphoria
without hearing any sound. Archive-ZER0 established containment to prevent
psychological contamination.

Logs:

Log 1 – Initial Observation
Date: 2021‑05‑05
Personnel Involved: Archivist Team Theta
Summary:
Chamber recorded harmonic vibrations undetectable by ear.
Observers exhibited emotional changes correlating with proximity.



Log 2 – Controlled Exposure
Date: 2021‑06‑18
Personnel Involved: Dr. F. Novak
Procedure: Sensor-monitored observation.
Findings:
Subjects displayed unpredictable emotional swings.
Containment reinforced with additional dampeners.



Log 3 – Incident Report Z-013-IR-02
Date: 2022‑01‑11
Personnel Involved: Research Team Lambda
Summary:
Unauthorized humming near chamber caused temporary
emotional instability in two observers. Protocols updated.
