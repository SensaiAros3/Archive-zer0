Z-009

Object Class: Eclipse ◎

Containment Status: Restricted Observation (Luminal Chamber)

Containment Procedures:
Z-009 is contained within a pitch-black chamber with no reflective surfaces.
Observers must wear insulated visual and auditory equipment.
Direct exposure to emitted light patterns is prohibited without protective filters.

Description:
The Luminous Echo is a floating sphere of radiant light that repeats
the emotional state of any observer in visual pulses.
Extended observation causes hallucinations of past traumas and memories.

Backstory:
Discovered in the depths of a cave system in Iceland in 2019, explorers
reported synchronized flashes that mirrored fear, elation, or sorrow.
Repeated exposure to the sphere induced vivid recollections and emotional
overload, necessitating containment by Archive-ZER0.

Logs:

Log 1 – Initial Discovery
Date: 2019‑07‑14
Personnel Involved: Field Team Omega
Summary:
Explorers observed the sphere emitting bright pulses mimicking their
emotions. Several team members fainted. Containment initiated.



Log 2 – Emotional Resonance Test
Date: 2019‑08‑02
Personnel Involved: Dr. R. Stein
Procedure: Observation under visual filters.
Findings:
The sphere projected complex emotional sequences,
eliciting involuntary physiological responses.



Log 3 – Incident Report Z-009-IR-03
Date: 2019‑09‑11
Personnel Involved: Research Team Beta
Summary:
Accidental removal of visual filters resulted in temporary
psychological breakdown for one researcher.
Containment protocols reinforced; mental health monitoring required.
